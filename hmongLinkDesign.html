Hmong Link

Skills:

  • Interview Skills
  • Figma
  • User Design & User Experience

Description:

This was made during my second semester of my User Experience Design course, as a solo project. The project was a semester long, and I decided to design a mobile application that helps people to learn Hmong. This was an important project to me as someone who is Hmong American and never had an easy access to learning my heritage language. For this UXD Project, I learned how to research my user base to define needs and requirements for the website, wireframing and prototyping in Figma, and interviewing with user testers to refine my design.
